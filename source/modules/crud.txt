What is CRUD?
-------------

    -Create
    -Read
    -Update
    -Delete

What functions does MongoDB use to perform CRUD operations?
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    -Create: db.collection.insert()
    -Read: db.collection.find()
    -Update: db.collection.update()
    -Delete: db.collection.remove(), db.collection.drop()
