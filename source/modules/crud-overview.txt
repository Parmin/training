What is CRUD?
-------------

- Create
- Read
- Update
- Delete

MongoDB CRUD Operations
-----------------------

- Create: :method:`db.collection.insert()`
- Read: :method:`db.collection.find()`
- Update: :method:`db.collection.update()`
- Delete: :method:`db.collection.remove()`, :method:`db.collection.drop()`
