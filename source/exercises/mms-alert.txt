Learning Objectives 
-------------------

- How to create an alert in MMS

.. include:: /includes/student-notes.rst


Exercise #1
-----------

Create an alert through MMS for any node within your cluster where total queues (reader and writer queues) average above 100 per second.  

The alert should send an email when it is triggered.
